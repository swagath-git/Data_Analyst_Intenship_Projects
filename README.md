# Bank Term Deposit Subscription Prediction

## 📖 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of my **Data Analyst Internship at SkillFied Mentor**. The task focuses on analyzing bank marketing data to understand customer behavior and to build a predictive, decision-support solution that estimates whether a customer is likely to subscribe to a **bank term deposit**.

The project emphasizes **business-oriented data analysis**, combining exploratory data analysis with basic predictive modeling to support better marketing decisions.

---

## 🧠 Business Problem

Banks run large-scale marketing campaigns for term deposits, but only a small percentage of customers respond positively. Inefficient targeting leads to higher costs and lower conversion rates.

### Objective

* Identify patterns and factors influencing customer subscription
* Support data-driven decision-making for marketing campaigns
* Improve campaign efficiency and customer targeting

---

## 📊 Dataset Description

* **File:** `data.csv`
* **Data Type:** Structured tabular data
* **Target Variable:** `y`

  * `yes` → Customer subscribed to a term deposit
  * `no` → Customer did not subscribe

### Feature Categories

* **Demographic:** age, job, marital status, education
* **Financial:** account balance, housing loan, personal loan
* **Campaign-related:** contact type, duration, number of contacts
* **Previous Campaign Outcome:** poutcome

## 📈 Key Business Insights

* Campaign **duration** has a strong influence on customer subscription behavior
* Customers with **successful previous campaign outcomes** show higher subscription probability
* Targeted campaigns based on data insights can significantly reduce marketing costs
